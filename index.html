Rockets

Electronics

Build and code electronics projects using platforms such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi






When and where do I show up?



Official meetings are held every Tuesday after school in the Makerspace, CC305. Project work often continues in the Makerspace during lunch and after school, so feel free to stop by to say hello or check out what we're doing.
